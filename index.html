Escape Road City 2 - Ultimate Police Chase Game

Escape Road City 2 is the exciting sequel to the popular action-escape game Escape Road City. This fast-paced racing game puts you in the driver's seat of a bank robber making a daring getaway from relentless police pursuit. Navigate challenging city roads, dodge obstacles, and master your driving skills to escape the police siege in this dramatic update to the escape road series.

Get ready to hit the streets in Escape Road City 2 to outsmart the law and become the ultimate escape artist! The game promises to bring players extremely satisfying experiences with chaotic high-speed chases through urban environments.

How to Play Escape Road City 2

Controls & Gameplay

  • Steering: Use arrow keys or swipe to control your car's direction
  • Acceleration: Press up arrow or tap to speed up
  • Braking: Use down arrow to slow down or reverse
  • Objective: Escape from police vehicles while avoiding obstacles
  • Strategy: Take advantage of terrain and shortcuts to lose your pursuers
  • Survival: Maintain your vehicle's health while navigating the challenging city roads

Game Features

Escape Road City 2 builds upon the original Escape Road experience with enhanced graphics, more challenging police AI, and a larger city environment to explore. Show off your driving skills in this intense escape road adventure where every decision could mean the difference between freedom and capture.
